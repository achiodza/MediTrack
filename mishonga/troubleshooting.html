Troubleshooting

If you're facing issues, please refer to the solutions below to help resolve them.

Common Issues

If you're unable to login, follow these steps:

  1. Ensure that your email and password are correct.
  2. If you've forgotten your password, use the Forgot Password link to reset it.
  3. Check your internet connection.
  4. Try clearing your browser cache and cookies.

If the application is running slowly, try the following:

  • Ensure that your internet connection is stable.
  • Close any unused tabs or applications that might be consuming system resources.
  • Clear your browser's cache and cookies.
  • Restart your device if the issue persists.

If the app isn't syncing data, try the following:

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Ensure that you are logged in with the correct account.
  • Log out and log back in to refresh the connection.
  • Ensure that syncing is enabled in the settings.
Still Need Help?
